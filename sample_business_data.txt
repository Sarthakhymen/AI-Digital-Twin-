SHARMA'S AUTHENTIC INDIAN KITCHEN — COMPANY PROFILE & MENU
============================================================

ABOUT US
--------
Sharma's Authentic Indian Kitchen is a family-owned restaurant established in 2018, located in the heart of Connaught Place, New Delhi. We specialize in North Indian, Mughlai, and Street Food cuisine. Our recipes have been passed down through four generations of the Sharma family, originating from the royal kitchens of Lucknow.

ADDRESS: Shop No. 45, Block C, Connaught Place, New Delhi - 110001

HOURS: Monday to Sunday, 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM
SEATING CAPACITY: 85 persons (Indoor: 60, Outdoor Terrace: 25)

OUR SPECIALTIES
---------------
We are famous for our hand-pulled Roomali Roti, slow-cooked Dal Makhani (cooked for 18 hours), and our signature Butter Chicken which uses a secret blend of 22 spices.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
---------------------------
Q: Do you have Jain food options?
A: Yes! We offer Jain-friendly versions of most dishes without onion and garlic. Please inform your server.

Q: Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?
A: Yes, our ground floor and restrooms are fully wheelchair accessible.

Q: Do you accept credit/debit cards?
A: Yes, we accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and cash.

Q: Can I book a private dining room?
A: Yes, we have a private dining area for up to 15 guests. Advance booking required. Call +91-9876543210.

Q: Do you offer vegetarian-only seating?
A: We have a dedicated vegetarian section in our restaurant for guests who prefer it.

Q: What is your cancellation policy for catering?
A: Cancellations made 7+ days before the event receive a full refund. Within 3-7 days: 50% refund. Less than 3 days: No refund.
